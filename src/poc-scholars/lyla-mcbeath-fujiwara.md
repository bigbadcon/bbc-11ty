Lyla McBeath Fujiwara is a Massachusetts-based TTRPG writer, game designer, and project lead. She's had many past lives, from teaching high schoolers in East Africa to eight years in tech, where she worked as a developer for Google News and created mobile programming online courses that taught hundreds of thousands of students. Her TTRPG work includes Jukebox: The Karaoke Musical Roleplaying Game (Coming October 2023 to Kickstarter), Encounters in the Radiant Citadel (DMsGuild), and Out of Luck (DMsGuild).
